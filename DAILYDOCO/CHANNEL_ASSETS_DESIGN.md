```yaml

- Monospace for alignment and clarity

Accent: Orbitron Medium
- Brand elements, futuristic emphasis
- Sparingly used for maximum impact
```

```yaml

- WebM: For web optimization
- Frame Rate: 30fps minimum, 60fps preferred
```
